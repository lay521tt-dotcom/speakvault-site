Culture note

Soft signals matter.

In NZ/AU workplace English, a phrase like “flag one area” can sound calmer than “point out a problem”. It keeps the tone collaborative while still being clear.

Giving a careful status update

我想说明进度有点慢，但我不想显得不负责任。

Natural

I wanted to give a quick update and be upfront about one thing that may take a little longer.

I’d like to share where things stand and flag one timeline risk before it becomes an issue.

cross-cultural soft disagreement

Disagreeing without closing the door

Team discussion where you see a risk in the proposed approach.

我不太同意这个方案，但我想留有余地。

Natural

I see the thinking behind it. I’m just wondering if there’s a lower-risk way to test it first.

Polished

I understand the rationale. My only hesitation is whether we can validate it on a smaller scale first.

“My only hesitation” makes disagreement specific and less confrontational.
