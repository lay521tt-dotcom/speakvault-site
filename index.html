The system

Not another word list. A speaking loop for real situations.

SpeakVault is designed around the moments when you already know what you mean in Chinese, but need English that sounds clear, natural, and professional.

01

Translate intent, not just words

Start from your Chinese thought and shape it into English for your role, tone, and situation.

02

Compare speaking styles

Choose from simple, natural, and more polished versions instead of memorising one rigid answer.

03

Keep a phrase vault

Save useful lines by meeting, interview, email, presentation, or everyday workplace context.

04

Practice before it matters

Turn saved phrases into short speaking drills so they feel ready when the conversation arrives.

How it works

A simple path from thought to spoken phrase.

Write it in Chinese

Start with the idea exactly as it appears in your head, without forcing a direct translation.

Choose the tone

Compare clear, natural, and polished options for the meeting, interview, or presentation ahead.

Save and rehearse

Keep the phrases that sound like you, then practise them until they feel ready to say aloud.

Examples

From a Chinese thought to workplace English with nuance.

SpeakVault helps you move beyond literal translation, so the same idea can become softer, clearer, or more confident depending on the room.

Chinese thought

我不是很确定这个方案是不是最优的，但我觉得我们可以先试一下。

Natural English

I’m not completely sure this is the best approach, but I think it’s worth testing first.

More polished

I have some reservations about whether this is the strongest option, but a small trial could help us decide.
